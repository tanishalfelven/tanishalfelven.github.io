Hello World

Welcome to tanishalfelven.github.io, you are laying your eyes on the first post, ever. Aren't you just a special cookie?

Now you might be wondering, where the in the nine hells have I found myself? I apologize if you don't mean to be here, and in the sad case that you are not a programmer (or some creature similar in design), you may show yourself out. Or stay. You might learn something.

Ah, but I've still avoided the question. This is my site. My domain1. This is where I lurk. This is where I'll post everything. The games I create, the tutorials for things I discover (Or perhaps just an improvement on what is already available), and of course this blog.

Now that sounds nice and dandy, but who exactly is this Half-Elf?

I am Tanis Half-Elven. I'm a programmer. I like to program. I love to program. I love to solve problems. I love to read other programming blogs. I like to find problems, and fix them. I like Open Source Work, and try to contribute to it when I've got the time. I like learning. I like to learn with other programmers, whether they are less or more experienced doesn't matter, as long as they have the work ethic. I like to spend long hours trying to figure out a bug from a simple division error, that is stupidly obvious once found2. I like to design interfaces, and fret about using sans-serif or serif fonts.

I enjoy all of this.

I am a programmer. I want to work hard, and continually create and learn.

This is where you can find me. This is my domain.
